## 3. Information Collection and Use

Crack The Code League provides **two gameplay modes**.

### ✅ Guest Player Mode

Guest Mode allows users to play without registration.

In this mode:

- No account creation is required
- No personal data is collected
- No data is stored on our servers
- No leaderboard participation is available
- Gameplay runs entirely on the local device
- All game data is deleted when the app is closed or uninstalled

**No tracking, analytics, or backend communication occurs in Guest Mode.**
